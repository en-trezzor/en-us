How to Set Up Your Trezor Wallet

Ready to start with Trezor? Follow these steps to set up your wallet and protect your digital assets.

Step 1: Go to Trézor.io/start

To begin, visit Trézor.io/start on your preferred browser. This official Trezor setup page provides all the instructions and tools needed to get your Trezor device ready.

Step 2: Select Your Trezor Model

Trezor offers two main models: the Trezor One and the Trezor Model T. The Model T includes a touchscreen and supports a broader range of cryptocurrencies, while the Trezor One is a reliable, affordable option. Choose the device that best fits your needs.

Step 3: Download Trezor Suite

Next, download the Trezor Suite software from Trézor.io/start. Available for Windows, macOS, and Linux, Trezor Suite is the platform through which you will manage your crypto assets. This software provides an organized dashboard to view balances, make transactions, and access advanced features.

Step 4: Install and Launch Trezor Suite

Once downloaded, install Trezor Suite on your computer and connect your Trezor device via USB. Launch the application, and follow the on-screen instructions to initialize your wallet. You’ll be prompted to set up a new wallet or restore an existing one using your recovery seed.

Step 5: Create a Secure Backup

During setup, Trezor will generate a recovery seed—a set of 12, 18, or 24 words that serve as a backup for your wallet. Write down these words and keep them in a secure place, as they’re the only way to restore your wallet if your Trezor device is lost or damaged. Never share this seed with anyone, as it provides access to your assets.
